Slip Ups

Every now and then you might slip up and have more cookies than planned! It happens to everyone and it is completely normal.

Slipping up occasionally, will not affect your progress to achieving your goals, yet your reaction to it will!

How we react to our slip ups can become a habit, so make it a good habit:

  • Replace negative thoughts with positive ones
  • Get curious; ask yourself why you did what you did
  • Regain control as soon as you can; prepare yourself a healthy meal or schedule your next workout
  • Get support from someone close to you
  • Focus on the progress you have already made
  • Think about how you can avoid this in the future
