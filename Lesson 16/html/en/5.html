Relapse

If slip ups are not kept in check, a person may begin to relapse and return to practicing older habits.
Address each slip up fully as they happen.

The process of relapsing looks something like this:

  1. Enter a high-risk situation
  2. No plan for how to cope
  3. Slip-up happens
  4. Negative thinking takes over
  5. No plan to move forward
  6. Another slip-up happens
  7. No plan for come back
  8. Full relapse
