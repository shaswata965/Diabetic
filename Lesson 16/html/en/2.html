Staying Motivated

Do you know what’s just as important as achieving your behavior change goals? Sustaining your behavior change.

It's easy to start but keeping it up is a challenge for many of us. Use these strategies keep yourself motivated:

  • Celebrate your success

    Try blogging about your achievements, having a reward, looking at “then and now” photos

  • Say thank-you

    Expressing gratitude to people who have helped you along the way to sustain the behavior

  • Support someone else

    When you help someone else on their journey, you may feel more encouraged to keep up with your own behavior.
