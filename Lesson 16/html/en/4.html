Coping with Negative Thoughts

It’s easy to end in a spiral of negative thoughts filled with guilt, sadness or stress. Will I ever reach my goal weight? Am I strong enough to do it? I shouldn’t have had that extra slice of cake.

Deal with those negative thoughts so they don’t prevent you from moving forward:

  • Share your feelings (fear, guilt, frustration…)
  • Ask for help
  • Identify your support system (family, friends, health care team, social services, spiritual advisors, etc.)
  • Try journal writing, humor, hobbies, physical activity, meditation, prayer, massage, reading, joining support groups…
