Lesson 2
Your Problem Solving Guide

Everyone has problems related to their health. Here’s a quick 5-step plan to help you address your problems head on!

  • Describe the problem in detail. Don’t be scared to get specific.

  • Brainstorm your options. Think about possible solutions.

  • Choose one option to try. Think about pros and cons.

  • Make a positive action plan. Set a goal that answers: When, What, Why, How?

  • Action! Did it work? If not, what went wrong? What other plan should I try?

Bottom line:

Problem solving is a process. Do not give up if you weren’t successful the first time.
