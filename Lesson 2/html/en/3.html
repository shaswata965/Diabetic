The Process of Change

  • Acknowledge the need for change

    1. You must believe it is needed
    2. Deal with any thoughts that are stopping you

  • Get ready to change

    1. Set goals
    2. Get informed
    3. Talk to others
    4. Think about how change will affect your life
    5. Choose behavior change goals

  • Take action

    1. Monitor progress
    2. Change the environment, if needed
    3. Substitute with healthy behavior, if needed

  • Achieve and maintain change
