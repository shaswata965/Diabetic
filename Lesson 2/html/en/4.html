Set a SMART Goal

Experts have discovered that the smaller and more specific a goal is, the better your chances of following through.

As you set goals in the coming months, think about these SMART guidelines:

  • Specific What am I going to do? Where?

  • Measurable How often? How far? For how long?

  • Achievable Challenging but doable. Think small.

  • Relevant Will it help with my diabetes?

  • Timed When will I start and reach my goal?
