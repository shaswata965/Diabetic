How to Get Support

The best strategy to obtain support is to turn it into a win-win situation by offering to do something for your support person.

  • Use ‘I’ statements better than ‘you’ statements. It is better to say that “ I feel annoyed when you say I should not eat this or that” instead of “You annoy me when you tell me I should not eat this or that.”

  • Acknowledge family’s concerns: This helps them feel valued.

  • Say what you need, offer to do something in return and set a time limit.

  • Thank your support person

  • Review the plan and renegotiate if you found the support to be helpful.
