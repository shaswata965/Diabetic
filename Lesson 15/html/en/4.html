Support from Professionals

You’re loved ones are not the only people you can seek support from.

Do not hesitate to use healthcare professionals for help:

  • Endocrinologists - Doctors specialized in diabetes, can help you with the medical side of diabetes.
  • Lifestyle coaches - Can help you adopt and sustain healthy habits
  • Nutritionists and Dieticians - Can help you with eating concernss
  • Pharmacists - Can help you understand the effects of medicine
  • Primary care provider - Can help you manage the disease and refer you to professionals
  • Psychologists and psychotherapists - Can help you cope with feelings and behaviors
  • Trainers and exercise psychotherapists - Can help you be active
  • Social Workers - Can provide you counseling and connect you with services
  • Recreational therapists - Can help you manage your daily life and be involved in communities

Droobi Tip

All these professionals are available for you, don’t be shy to ask for referral. You never know how that can change your life.
