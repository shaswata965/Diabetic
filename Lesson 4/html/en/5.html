Ways to Get Active

Building more activity as part of your lifestyle will make it more likely that you will continue the behavior!

Tips to get more active:

  • Dance to your favorite music

  • Play actively with your children or pets

  • Replace Friday drives with Friday walks

  • Take a walk after dinner with your family

  • Track your steps. Work up to 10,000 steps / day.

  • Walk around whenever you talk on the phone.

  • Walk briskly when you shop.

  • Take the stairs at work

  • Stand up when you watch TV, move during the ads
