Stay F.I.T.T

Different kinds of exercises have different effects on your body.

Follow F.I.T.T. Aerobic exercises to help you strengthen your heart.

F.I.T.T stands for:

  • Frequency -How often?

    Try to be active on most days. Your best option is to be active around 5-7 days a week. Start with what you can!

  • Intensity - How hard?

    Aerobic exercises (walking, swimming…) will move your whole body. Start with a low intensity then increase it as tolerated.

  • Time -How long?

    Stay active for at least 10 minutes at a time. Slowly increase it! Droobi has already calculated your activity targets, follow-it!

  • Type of activity - What kind?

    Do what you like, but also try something new! Aerobic (walking, swimming, jogging…), Resistance (weightlifting), Flexibility (yoga), and Balance (lower body exercise).
