Why is it important to be active?

Exercise is beneficial for you in many ways, including:

  • Lowers blood pressure and maintains heart health

  • Reduces your “bad” LDL cholesterol levels and increases your “good” HDL cholesterol.


  • Increases energy and boosts mood.


  • Burns calories to help you lose or maintain weight

  • Helps you sleep better

  • Strengthens your muscles, bones and keeps your joints flexible

  • Lowers blood glucose levels and Reducing insulin resistance
