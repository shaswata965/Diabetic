What is Your Physical Activity Plan

Physical Activity is an activity that gets you moving. Exercise refers to a specific, planned physical activity such as walking, swimming, biking...

A physical activity plan should answer the following questions

What exercise is safe & practical for you?

When and Where will you do these activities?

How often and for how long will you exercise?

It is important to select activities you enjoy!

Please remember to consult your doctor before setting up your physical activity plan.
