How to monitor blood glucose?

Here are the steps for monitoring your blood glucose:

  • Always wash your hand very well

  • Take a strip out from the container

  • Insert the strip into your glucose meter

  • Put a new lancet into your lancing device every time

  • Prick the side of your finger to take a drop of blood

  • Apply blood drop on the strip

  • Read the glucose meter and put the reading into your Droobi application
