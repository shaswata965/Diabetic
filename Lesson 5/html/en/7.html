Handling the Glucometer

Lancets are used to prick your skin in order to get a blood sample.

  • Place your used lancets in a shatter proof or a “medical sharps” container
  • Do not throw it out in the garbage.
  • Send the full sharp box to the nearest Primary Health Care Center (PHCC

Be kind to your glucose meter.Do not expose it to very cold or hot temperatures.

Ensure proper storage of your test strips!Keep them in their original vial. Close the vial tightly always.

Remember to check the expiry date on the vial and discard it 9 months after you first open it.
