What is a healthy range for blood glucose?

Your personal blood glucose target depends different factors.

If you donâ€™t have diabetes, target:

  1. Fasting blood glucose: 70-100 mg/dL
  2. Blood glucose 2 hours after meal: less than 140 mg/dL
  3. Hemoglobin A1C: Less than 6.0%

When you have diabetes, target:

  1. Fasting blood glucose: 90-130 mg/dL
  2. Blood glucose 2 hours after meal: less than 180 mg/dL
  3. Hemoglobin A1C: Less than 7%

Droobi Tip

Alternate your testing before different meals, to get a better understanding of your blood glucose during the day!
