When to check your blood glucose levels?

There is no standard guideline for how often you should monitor your blood glucose levels. You will decide that with your healthcare team.

Make sure every blood glucose monitor done is meaningful!

Suggested times to test your blood glucose levels:

  • Fasting - Before you eat or drink anything
  • Before a meal
  • Two hours after a meal
  • Bedtime - at night
  • Pregnant women should generally monitor 1 or 2 hours after meal.

Depending on your treatment, it may be beneficial also to test blood glucose levels:

  • Before, during and after physical activity
  • When having symptoms of low/high blood sugar
  • During times of illness and stress
  • During any medication change
  • Driving/ traveling for long distances.
