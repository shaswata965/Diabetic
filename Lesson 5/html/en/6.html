Get Adequate Blood Sample

  • Use warm water to wash your hands. This helps increase blood circulation to the fingertips.

  • Shake your hands to force the blood to the fingertips.

  • Get a blood sample from the side of your fingertips. This is less painful.

  • Gently milk blood from the bottom toward the fingertip to get a blood sample.
