In Case of Emergencies

When to call the doctor

Keep a “sick-day-box” including supplies from your healthcare provider, the provider's phone number, and a list of sick-day rules.

Call the doctor or diabetes hotline 10699 when:

  • You have persistent vomiting and cannot keep anything down
  • Have persistent diarrhea more than 5 times or longer than 6 hours
  • Have a persistent fever 38.3 C or higher
  • Have blood glucose level above 300 mg/dl
  • Have moderate or large urine ketones

Be prepared!

Be prepared for an emergency by following these guidelines:

A. Ensure that you have Food & Water: 7-10 day supply on hand

B. Ensure that you always have extra two weeks supply medications.

C. Keep an updated list of all medications handy (dose, frequency, provider prescribing)

D. Make an emergency help information card
