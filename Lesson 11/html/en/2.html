Sick day action plan: Monitor Glucose Levels

Illness, especially infection, can raise your blood glucose levels.

Test your glucose levels more frequently on sick days! Let your healthcare provider know if your blood glucose levels are constantly out of target during times of illness.

→ Did you know…?

As part of the body’s defense mechanism for fighting illness and infection, more glucose is released into the blood vessels. This will increase your blood sugars even if you are off your food or eating less than usual.
