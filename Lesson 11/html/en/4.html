Sick day Action Plan: Food, Hydration, Medication

Keep Eating

On a sick day, your appetite might be affected by the illness. If you cannot eat your regular meals, get your nutrients from liquids and soft foods.

If you are on insulin or sulfonylureas keep eating, even if not feeling well, small amounts of carbohydrates.

Stay Hydrated

On sick days, it is important to stay hydrated. Fluids might be lost through increased urination due to hyperglycemia, sweating due to fever, vomiting or diarrheaâ€¦

Drink at least one cup of calorie-free liquids (mostly water) each hour.

Caffeine can make you urinate more often, so it is best to avoid coffee and tea.

Take your medication

Do NOT stop taking your diabetes medication on sick days without consulting your healthcare provider.

Even if you are not eating much, your illness will increase the blood sugar levels. Continue taking your medication!
