Stress

Stress will always come up every now and then, and it’s okay! As long as you deal with it in a way that doesn’t negatively impact your health.

Any kind of change, small or big, positive or negative, can cause stress. It could be conflicts at work or home, health, Finances, New job or baby, Duties, Wedding, Vacation…

Think about the last time you were stressed and how you coped with it.

You may be stressed if you’re experiencing one or more of the following:

  • Moodiness or irritability
  • Feeling overwhelmed
  • Loneliness or isolation
  • Memory problems
  • Inability to concentrate
  • Aches and pains
  • Diarrhea, constipation
  • Nausea, dizziness
  • Eating more or less
  • Sleeping too much or too little
  • Withdrawing from others
