Depression

If stress is left untreated for a long time, it can lead to common psychological disorders like depression and anxiety.

Depression is common amongst people living with diabetes:

  • 12.5% of people with diabetes have major depression
  • 20% may have less severe depression
  • Untreated depression makes managing diabetes a lot harder than it is.

Symptoms

  • Persistent sadness for longer than 2 weeks
  • Feelings of hopelessness, guilt, worthlessness, helplessness
  • Loss of interest in activities you used to enjoy
  • Decreased energy
  • Difficulty concentrating, remembering, making decisions
  • Sleeping problems (difficulty sleeping or oversleeping)
  • Appetite and/or weight changes
  • Restlessness or irritability
  • Thoughts of death or suicide, or suicide attempts
