Blood Pressure and cholesterol

Blood pressure

For adults with diabetes the recommended target for blood pressure is less than 130/80 mmHg.

At every routine visit to your healthcare provider, your blood pressure should be measured.

LDL levels

There are two types of cholesterol.

  • LDL cholesterol is the “bad” type, which can lead to blocked blood vessels.

  • HDL cholesterol is the “Good” type, which can help protect your heart.

If your LDL levels are above 100 mg/dL, you should start lifestyle changes as a first step.
