Neuropathy (Nerve disease)

Neuropathy is a disease of the nervous system. Nerves are present throughout the body to allow movement, sensation or automatic functions like heartbeat, bladder function…

Risks

About 60-70% of people with diabetes have mild to severe forms of neuropathy.

Prevention

  • Aim for a healthy body weight
  • Limit alcohol consumption
  • People with type 2 diabetes should be screened for diabetic peripheral neuropathy at diagnosis and repeated each year
  • Those with type 1 diabetes should begin annual screenings 5 years after diagnosis
  • People with neuropathy should have a visual inspection of their feet each visit with their provider
  • Tell your provider about any abnormal feelings in your feet, legs, or hands (tingling, burning, numbness…)
  • Stop smoking and exercise regularly
