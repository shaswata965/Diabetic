Peripheral Neuropathy (Foot Care)

One part of the body where nerve damage can occur is the feet, called peripheral neuropathy.

Risk

When nerves are exposed to high level of glucose for too long, it can begin to break down. About half of the people with diabetes who have nerve damage to the feet, feel no symptoms.

Prevention

  • Keep your feet clean and dry
  • Inspect your feet between your toes daily
  • Never walk barefoot
  • Make your habit of cutting your toenails to follow the curve of your toe
  • Wear only comfortable, well-fitting shoes
  • Check the inside of your shoes every time
  • Report to your provider any change in color, sensation, cuts or open sores.
