Nephropathy (Kidney disease)

High blood glucose and high blood pressure can damage the kidneys; when the kidneys are damaged, they can no longer remove waste and extra fluids from the bloodstream, and protein leaks into the urine.

Risk

Of all people with diabetes, around 30% have nephropathy.

Prevention

  • Keep your blood glucose level at target. People who tightly control their diabetes reduce their risk of kidney disease by 35-56%
  • Keep your blood pressure at target.
