Skin Care

Your skin is your bodyâ€™s first line of defense. Any breaks in the skin put you at risk for infection. If your glucose level is elevated, the risk for infection is increased.

Risk

About one-third of people with diabetes will have a skin disorder related to diabetes. Almost one-third of people with diabetes have severe gum disease.

Prevention

  • Keep your skin clean
  • Dry well after bathing
  • Avoid very hot baths and showers if you have loss of sensation
  • Prevent dry skin
  • Drink lots of water
  • Treat cuts quickly
