Here are simple rules to follow to eat well for your diabetes:

  • Have 3 meals every day, including breakfast.

  • Space your meals 4 to 6 hours apart. When you skip or delay a meal, your blood glucose level can go low or high. Both not good!

  • If you eat snacks between meals, keep them small.

  • Be mindful of portions; use smaller plates and cups.

  • Have a variety from different food groups at each meal: Vegetables & Fruits, Grains & Starches, Milk & Alternatives, Meat & Alternatives

  • Eat more vegetables.

  • Limit foods high in sugar (desserts) and use less fat.

  • When thirsty, choose water. Limit sodas and juice.
