Tools for Healthy Eating: Portion Control

Thinking ahead and planning meals/snacks is the key to healthy eating and also the best way to lose weight.

Planning helps manage the budget, makes shopping easier and maximizes foods that are high in nutrients.

While we all need the same kinds of nutrients, how much we need will differ from person to person.

You can use everyday objects or your hand to judge the size of a portion.

  • A Fist or 1 Tennis ball = 1 cup of cereal, pasta, rice…
  • 2 handfuls = 2 cups vegetables…
  • A palm of hand or a deck of cards = 90g meat, chicken…
  • One finger = 1 serving of cheese…
  • A thumb Tip = 1 Teaspoon oil
