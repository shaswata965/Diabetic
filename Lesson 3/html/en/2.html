Why is healthy eating important?

Eating healthy is an important part of leading a healthy lifestyle.

Having a nutritious diet will help you:

  • Keep your blood glucose, blood pressure, and cholesterol levels at target

  • Attain and maintain a healthy body weight

  • Provide adequate nutrition to your body

  • Prevent or delay diabetes-related complications

  • Feel good and have more energy

  • Help you decrease or delay medication intake
