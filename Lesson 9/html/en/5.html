Hyperglycemia

Hyperglycemia is when your blood glucose levels are above your normal target range.

Symptoms include

  • Frequent urination
  • Thirst
  • Blurred Vision
  • Excessive hunger

Some people might not feel any symptoms!

Reasons include

  • Delaying or stopping medication
  • Eating excessive foods rich in carbohydrates
  • Gaining weight
  • Stopping regular physical activity
  • Becoming ill or stressed
  • Not sleeping well at night
  • Only testing blood glucose based on ‘how you feel’
