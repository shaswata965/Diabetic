Hyperglycemia - Treatment and Prevention

In order to treat hyperglycemia, you must:

  • Identify probable reason(s) for it

  • Take your medication as directed by your doctor

  • Hydrate yourself by drinking more water

  • Get physically active

    Regular exercise is often an effective way to control your blood sugar. However, don't exercise if blood sugars are very high, it can drive your blood sugar even higher.

  • Check your blood sugar levels regularly

  • Change your eating habits
