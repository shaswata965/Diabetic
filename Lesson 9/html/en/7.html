Hyperglycemia Prevention

  • Stay at blood glucose target

  • Take diabetes medications as prescribed- be mindful of dose, timing, storage, expiry and delivery

  • Consult health care team for illness or infection

  • Test blood for glucose and urine for ketones as directed, especially during illness or stress

  • Discuss blood glucose results regularly with health care team

  • Follow your prescribed meal plan

  • Follow a regular pattern of physical activity

  • Do not delay intervening!
