Hypoglycemia – Treatment and Prevention

Hypoglycemia can be hard on the heart and brain. Please treat immediately when it happens. Do not delay!

Follow “The rule of 15” to effectively treat hypoglycemia.

  • When feeling unwell, check your blood glucose level immediately. If below 70 mg/dl TREAT!

  • Have 15 g of fast acting sugar. (4 glucose tablets, 120ml fruit juice or regular soft drink, 3 sugar teaspoons

  • Wait 15 minutes, then check your blood glucose levels again. If it is still below target, repeat step 2

  • If it is within range (>70 mg/dl), Take 1 carbohydrate + protein snack (1 toast + cheese or 1 cup yogurt or 1 fruit + 10 almonds…) if the next meal is delayed more than 1 hour or wait for the meal if it’s in less than 1 hour

Hypoglycemia can be prevented by:

✔ Eating meals / snacks on time.
✔ Including carbohydrates at meals as planned
✔ Taking your diabetes medication as prescribed –right dose at the right time.
✔ Planning for intense physical activity sessions.

Inform someone about your hypoglycemia, how to recognize it and how to treat it.
