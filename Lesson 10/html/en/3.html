Check Blood Sugar

If you're planning a long workout or a new activity:

  • Check your blood sugar every 30 minutes while exercising
  • Also, check your blood sugar when you finish exercising
  • And several times during the next few hours

Exercise draws on sugar reserves stored in your muscles and liver. As your body rebuilds these stores, it takes sugar from your blood

We know it is not easy to keep checking your glucose level, but it will prevent further complications
