Exercising with Complications

If you have any of the following diabetes-related complications, you must be cautious with your exercise.

  • Heart Disease

    Peripheral Neuropathy

    Autonomic Neuropathy

    Retinopathy

    Peripheral Vascular Disease

Before you begin any exercise program, you must consult your healthcare provider

When living with complications, AVOID strenuous exercise, heavy lifting and straining, isometric exercise, high-impact activities… done in extreme heat or cold

AIM for walking, daily chores, gardening, moderate lifting, walking, cycling, swimming, chair exercises, stretching… done in moderate climate
