Tips to Prevent Hypoglycemia

  • Carry carbohydrates to treat low blood sugar

  • Rehearse plan for treating hypoglycemia

  • Educate coaches and teammates about signs, symptoms, and treatment of hypoglycemia

What about insulin:

If you take insulin, you should know its action. Physical activity at the peak time of the insulin can cause hypoglycemia (low blood glucose). Some adjustments might be required.
