How to Cope with Challenges

There can be many reasons why we don’t think we can workout. Maybe it’s a lack of time or the weather might be too hot. Here are ways to overcome these challenges so that you can be prepared!

  • Work out in your own home or area.
  • Break your 150 minutes into smaller chunks and Use a fitness app.
  • Park your car farther away from the place you want to go.
  • Take stairs instead of elevator.
  • Do sit-ups or pedal a stationary bike while you watch TV.
  • Plant and care for a vegetable or flower garden.
  • Take a walk during break
  • Plan the activity with a friend
  • Choose activities that won't aggravate physical ailments.
  • Consider doing seated or water exercise
  • It is possible to do resistance exercises sitting down as well.
  • Be active with your kids.
  • Ask for help.
  • Use gym child care.
  • Work out indoors (mall, stairs, grocery store isles…).
  • Dress for the weather.
  • Work out in your own home.
  • Work out at a gym or community center.
  • Walking is free
  • Buy workout clothes and equipment on sale.
  • Look for free fitness classes at your library or community center.
  • Dance, listen to music, watch TV show…
  • Play with your kids
  • Work out with a friend
  • Use a fitness app
  • Keep trying new things until you find something you like
