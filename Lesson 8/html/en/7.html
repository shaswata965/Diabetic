Tips

  • Talk with your healthcare team about medications

  • Respect time, dose and storage of medications

  • Keep it at convenient locations and use self-reminders

    • Droobi Calendar
    • Sticky notes
    • Medicine organizer
    • Cell phone reminders

  • Discuss with your healthcare team about travel plan or when medication is missed or damaged

  • If money is an issue, ask for the less expensive generic form of the medication or reach out to Qatar Diabetes Association for assistance
