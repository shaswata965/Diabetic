Injection rules to follow

Insert the needle at a 90 degrees angle

  • For 4-5 mm needles, no pinching required
  • For 6, 8 and 12mm needles, a skin fold is recommended

Inject in the fat layer between the skin and the muscle:

  • The back of the upper arm
  • Tops outer part of the thigh
  • Outer part of the buttock
  • Abdomen (most recommended)

A small blood drop or a bruise are common, no worries!

Never reuse the same needle. Discard used ones in a shatter-proof sharp box

Avoid injecting into muscles, veins, scar tissues

Stay away 2 inches from the belly button
