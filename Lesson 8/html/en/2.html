How to Inject Using an Insulin Pen

Insulin is an effective medication for diabetes control. If you are taking it, you will find this information very useful!

To perform your insulin injection:


  • Wash your hands and clean your injection site

  • Put a new needle onto your pen

  • Remove the caps of the pen needle

  • Hold the pen upright and perform an ‘air shot’, by dialing up 2 units and pressing the plunger for a test shot of insulin needle. This will clear any bubbles in the needle.

  • Dial up your dose

  • Pick a soft fatty injection site

  • Put the needle in and keep the pen steady

  • Push the plunger slowly to inject the dose

  • Hold the needle in for 10 seconds to deliver insulin

  • Ensure that the used needle is deposited into a sharps bin.
