Site Rotation and insulin storage

Site Rotation

Rotate injection sites within the same body area for 1-2 weeks, and then move to a new injection area. This will prevent developing fatty lumps under the skin (lipohypertrophy) and will ensure consistent insulin absorbency.

Insulin Storage

  • Unopened vials should be kept refrigerated between 2-8 degrees Celsius.
  • Storing insulin in the desert: Keep it inside the wet sand if the ice box or refrigerator not available.
  • Discard insulin:
    • after its expiry date, even if unused
