How to Draw and Inject Insulin?

Preparing and taking injections using a syringe and Vial:

  • Wash your hands and clean your injection site

  • Use alcohol to clean the rubber top of the vial

  • Draw air into the syringe- same as the dose of the medicine you need from the vial

  • With the vial upright, stick the needle into the rubber top of the vial. Inject air into the vial.

  • Hold the vial and syringe together firmly and turn it upside down

  • Slowly draw insulin into the syringe

  • If you see air bubbles, slowly inject back into the vial and redraw. Repeat until no bubbles are seen and your dose is correct

  • Remove needle from vial

  • Gently pinch up a layer of fat, quickly insert the needle into the skin and inject

  • If you see air bubbles, slowly inject back into the vial and redraw. Repeat until no bubbles are seen and your dose is correct
