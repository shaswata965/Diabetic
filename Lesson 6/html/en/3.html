MyPlate Food Groups: Starches and Protein

A quarter of your plate can be filled with starches. Choose whole grain more often and look for products with more fiber.

This section of the plate can include a variety of whole grains like: whole wheat flour and pasta, oatmeal, brown rice, jareesh, harees, rye, barley, buckwheat…

It also includes Starchy vegetables like corn, parsnip, potato, pumpkin, squash… And legumes like beans, foul, chickpeas, lentils, peas…

  • Fish: include 2-3 times per week
  • Chicken: Remove skin for less fat
  • Beef: choose lean and extra lean cuts- round, flank, chuck, or rump roast
