How to Cope with Challenges

We have all had discouraging thoughts about making changes. Here are some ways to deal with the most common thoughts that people face with eating healthier.

“I’ll never be able to change how I eat”

Set Simple, Measurable, Achievable, Realistic and Timely goals. One change at a time! Eating two medium fruits a day is an easy goal to reach. Giving up your favorite food is much harder, and you will be more likely to not even try.

”I don't have time to make changes”

  • Find time-management techniques that work for you.
  • Ask others how they manage to fit good nutrition into their lives.
  • Don't try to make too many changes at once. Small changes take less time, but they add up.
  • Ask your family and friends for support.
  • Cook quick meals and easy recipes.
