MyPlate Food Groups:Vegetables

As mentioned in lesson 3, MyPlate is an easy-to-follow guideline to healthy eating. Aim to eat from all food groups every day. This will give your body the nutrients it needs to stay healthy.

Half of your plate should be filled with vegetables! Vegetables are full of vitamins, minerals and fiber. They have few calories so you can enjoy more!

Remember potatoes and corn do not count as vegetables.

  • Choose a variety of colors, fresh, frozen or even canned.
  • Try different cooking methods and seasoning
