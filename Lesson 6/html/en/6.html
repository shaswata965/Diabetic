What Can I Drink?

Food is often a focus when it comes to diabetes, but don't forget that the beverages you drink can also affect your weight and blood glucose!

It’s important to stay hydrated and water is simply your best choice when it comes to hydration.

Try adding ice, flavors, mint, ginger, berries…for a change in flavor!

What to avoid?

Avoid sugary drinks like regular soda, fruit punch, fruit drinks, energy drinks, sports drinks, sweet tea, and other sugary drinks. These will raise blood glucose and can provide several hundred calories in just one serving!
