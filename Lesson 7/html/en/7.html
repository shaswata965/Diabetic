Insulin Pump Therapy

An insulin pump is an external device that delivers insulin continuously from a refillable storage reservoir by means of a cannula placed under the skin. It is mostly used by people living with type 1 Diabetes.

Some of the benefits of using an insulin pump include:

Improving quality of life

Reducing frequency and severity of hypoglycemia

Tighter glycemic control, decreasing the risks of long-term complications.

Improved convenience, flexibility and lifestyle.

Reducing basal rates during low physiologic requirements may lessen the frequency.

Improved convenience, flexibility, satisfaction and lifestyle.

Insulin needs can be tailored to accommodate changes in schedules.

Droobi Tip

It can take time to adjust diabetes. After all, diabetes requires finger pricking, blood testing, shot-giving and self-monitoring to reach and maintain normal blood sugar levels. Be patient to fine-tune your blood glucose!
