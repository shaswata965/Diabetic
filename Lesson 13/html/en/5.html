Consider Other Actions

  • Obesity Screening

    Weight management is an important health issue.
    Your body weight should be measured at each diabetes visit, and Body Mass Index (BMI) calculated using weight and height.
    Weight loss is recommended for all adults overweight (BMI 25.9-29.9) or obese (BMI greater than or equal to 30).

  • Vaccination

    If you have diabetes, you should have all routine vaccinations recommended. You should also have an annual influenza & pneumonia vaccine.
    If you are between 19 and 59, consider Hepatitis B vaccination.
