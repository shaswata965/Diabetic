Get your ABC in check!

  • A1C:

    Get your A1C checked every 3-6 months. This will give you a good reference about your diabetes management.
    Aim for a target of 7% or less.

  • Blood pressure:

    Get your Blood Pressure checked when seeing your doctor, or also at home if you have a monitor.
    Aim for 130/80 mmHg or less.

  • Cholesterol:

    Make sure you get your LDL (“bad cholesterol”) checked at least once a year.
    Aim for less than 100 mg/dL.
