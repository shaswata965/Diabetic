Smoking Cessation

It is important to include smoking cessation as part of your diabetes management and healthy lifestyle changes.

There are many programs (e.g. telephone help lines) and tools (e.g. gums and patches) available to assist with cessation efforts.

Where to startâ€¦

  • Set a quit date.

  • Let friends and family know they can help you


  • Change your environment

    Get rid of all cigarettes, lighters, and ashtrays at work, home, or in your car

  • List reasons for quitting

    Remind yourself of those reasons every day

  • Identify smoking triggers

    Make a plan to deal with them.

  • Find ways to cope with the withdrawal from nicotine

    Medicine, nicotine replacement, and behavior modification can help.

  • Ask for smoking cessation medication and referral to smoking clinic.

  • If you slip up, set a new quit date and try again.

    Some people need several tries before they quit smoking.
