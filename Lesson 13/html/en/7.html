How to Cope with Challenges

  • Make your doctor a partner in care.

    Take responsibility for your care, and don't leave everything to your doctor. One way to do this is to listen to your body and track its changes.

  • Build a team.

    Reach out to a healthcare team with experts. A nurse can help you stop smoking, a dietitian will give you the best nutrition information.

  • Coordinate your care.

    Get all the information from your primary care physician

  • Make a healthy investment in yourself.

    Implement smoking cessation, weight loss, more physical activity and healthy eating.
    Investing time and energy to make healthy changes is worth it! You can feel immediate benefits of feeling better and having more energy, as well as long-term benefits like increased life expectancy.
